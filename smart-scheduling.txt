Purpose:
The Smart Scheduling module ensures that drivers and riders receive the most efficient ride time suggestions.

Inputs:
- Driver availability
- Rider availability
- Class schedule
- Historical ride data (optional)

Outputs:
- Suggested meeting time
- Conflict alerts
- Reminders and notifications

Edge Cases:
- Overlapping classes
- Driver running late
- Rider canceling last minute
