Key App Features

  • Verified Student Profiles

    • DriveBuddy verifies every user using their official university email. Drivers may optionally submit safety documents (insurance, license, etc.) to ensure a secure and trusted carpooling environment.

    Smart Scheduling

    Smart Scheduling automatically analyzes class schedules, preferred travel times, and route patterns to suggest the most optimal ride times. It detects schedule conflicts, sends ride reminders, and adapts to changes in real-time.

    • Detects schedule conflicts instantly
    • Suggests ideal pickup times based on class locations
    • Sends notifications for upcoming rides
    • Automatically syncs with student’s weekly timetable

In-App Chat & Rating System

Riders and drivers can communicate instantly inside the app to coordinate pickups, share status updates, and resolve questions. After each ride, both users can leave ratings and safety feedback to maintain accountability and trust.

  • Real-time messaging for quick coordination
  • Rating system for both drivers and riders
  • Built-in safety issue reporting
  • Chat history stored securely

Eco-Friendly Incentives

DriveBuddy rewards sustainable commuting. Every shared ride earns “Green Points,” which track carbon savings and fuel efficiency. These points may be redeemed for campus discounts or rewards.

  • Earn points for every eco-friendly shared ride
  • Tracks carbon reduction and fuel saved
  • Unlock eco badges as you progress
  • Potential rewards through university partnerships

Route Optimization

Dynamic route suggestions ensure that drivers take the most efficient path. If a nearby rider needs pickup, drivers receive an optional match request.

Recurring or One-Time Carpools

Users can schedule repeating daily or weekly carpools, or request a single ride when needed. Smart Scheduling helps automate both options.
